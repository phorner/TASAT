DEPOT/CORRIDOR (1511-A686354-F) Date: 221-1117 
The Naval High Command of Corridor Fleet announced the arrival of Emperor Lucan's Transfer Order. 

To help save the Imperium from the rebel Dulinor, the announcement said, Corridor Fleet is to transfer to Zarushagar sector within a month. 

Local authorities expressed confidence that sufficient assets would remain in Corridor sector to defend the region against Vargr raids. [Challenge 33]
