AMDANI/DAIBEI (2014-A727A88-E) Date: 341-1117 
Imperial forces retreating ahead of the advancing Solomani Fleet made a stand this week at Landumash and Amdani, and fought the Solomani to a stand- still. 

A naval spokesperson expressed a feeling of confidence that the invaders would be forced from Daibei sector by the end of the year. [Challenge 33]
