HINDAHL/OLD EXPANSES (1422-A754A86-B) Date: 224-1117 
Sector naval headquarters announced the transfer of the Old Expanses Fleet to Zarushagar for operations against the usurper, Dulinor. The brief announcement from headquarters indicated that all the first-line fleets would be transferred within 60 days. 

"The defense of the sector lies in the capable hands of our reserve squadrons," Sector Admiral Hinchcliffe said at the press conference which revealed the arrival of the Transfer Order from Emperor Lucan. [Challenge 33]
