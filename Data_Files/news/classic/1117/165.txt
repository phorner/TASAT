TERRA/SOL (0207-A867A69-F) Date: 165-1117 
The combined Solomani fleet has overrun fleets from the Albadawi and Dingir subsectors. Elements of both fleets are engaged in a large outer system battle at Dingir itself. 

Popular support for Solomani reclamation of the sector is becoming apparent in several systems. Increased incidences of rioting, protesting, and sabotage plague the war effort on Terra and other worlds. [Challenge 31]
