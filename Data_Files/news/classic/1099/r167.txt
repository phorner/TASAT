167-1099 Denotam/Spinward Marches

The Glas, head of Denotam's Clan Glas, claims that she has obtained copies of personal logs and diaries of Adm. (retired) Aramais P. Lee written during his eight years as a special advisor of the IISS (1089-1097).  The Glas claims that Lee was taking action on behalf of the private shipping corporation Sharurshid as early as 1091, and continuing to the present day.

Lee is rumored to be currently negotiating trade arrangements for Sharurshid elsewhere in the sector.
