Quare/Vilis (SM) Date: 232-1107

Authorities at Quare today issued the following advisory to all shipping in the Quare system.

+ + + NAVIGATION HAZARD WARNING + + +
Two Knnn ships are currently active in the Quare system. While they have made no approach to any of the system's planets, they have shown the usual Knnn disregard for traffic regulations. Ships' masters are advised to avoid contact with the Knnn vessels. No attempt to communicate with them should be made, due to the unpredictability of their reactions.
