CAPITAL/CORE Date: 166-1118 
In a sudden and rare move, the Palace announced a public execution, that of accused traitor Duke Anthony. 

The execution took place in the security block, and minimal coverage was allowed. 

Lucan then ordered the absolute destruction of Anthony's family estate and lands, carried out by the palace guards later in the day with plasma weapons fire. 

The duke's family has been deported and shamed, allowed to travel to a second family home which is located in Ilelish sector. 

Inside sources claim that Lucan went into his rage when the panel he appointed personally was about to find the defendant not guilty. [Challenge 37]
