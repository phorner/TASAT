CAPITAL/CORE Date: 019-1118 
Large numbers of Dulinor's ships have broken through into Dagudashaag and Core sectors, apparently with the intention of disrupting interworld trade routes. 

The Admiralty released a communique in which it discussed plans to mobilize more reserve units and pull several mainline ships out of the front to deal with this new problem. The communique also assures that the penetrating ships have minimal ground attack capabilities. 

All transport craft of any kind are encouraged to travel in organized, protected convoys whenever possible. [Challenge 36]
