312-1098
Nojityt/Antares 2308

Imperial Navy Lieutenant Commander Niit Brzk was today awarded the Medal for Conspicuous Gallantry for his actions in command of the frigate Unekhzektha in the Tekahiu system. Admiral Marcus Singh, commander of the 290th Fleet was overheard in conversation with an aide joking that she "wished more of her officers had the akghekhs [courage] of Brzk."
