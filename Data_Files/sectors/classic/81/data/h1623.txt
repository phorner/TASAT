Velpare

Known throughout Core sector for its fine cuisine.  The world's chefs cook a variety of delicacies, all prepared from native plant and animal life.  One spice in particular, the varinte plant, is highly prized for its sweet, pungent flavor, and can be bought as far away as Vland.

Velpare is the subsector capital.  The Count of Frethe, an influential noble in the Moot holding thou-sands of proxy votes, hails from here.

The scout base is small, but is an important link in the X-boat network, overseeing operations in a five-parsec radius.
