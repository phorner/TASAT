World: Ruies

Ref'iib Homeworld
Population: 3,462,911

This is the homeworld of the Ref'iib, a minor alien race. They are just beginning their reach into space, and have found themselves landlocked by the Vargr worlds around them.


This system has a second habitable planet. It too is a moon of the same gas giant as the main world. Reais H - F510263-5, it is a colony of the main world established only 20 years ago.
