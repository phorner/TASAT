Joelii  2915

With Pliska(2916) and Izir(2917), Joelii forms the
 Conclave of Joelii. The world of Joelii is the government center for the
 Conclave and all representives from Pliska live here until they retire or 
are voted out of office.
Izir is represented by people selected by GenAssist 
without regard to the Iziri populance wishes.
