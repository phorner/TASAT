Antiquity

Featured in TD3. The First Survey discovered an Ancient Site here. It proved to be the only site found in working condition.

Monadium
Impenetrable bluish-gray metal used as the basic construction material by the Ancients at the site on Antiquity. It has defeated all efforts to determine its exact composition; some experts believe it contains titanium. Even modern TL 15 plasma and fusion weapons have been unable to penetrate it.
