
While technically still in operational condition, the starport is deserted. It is estimated that several billion credits are needed to repair the exterior of dozen of buildings in the vast complex.

This world was deserted during the Long Night. In the early years of the Third Imperium, a wealthy noble completely restored the old First Imperium era starport complex, and opened it to tourists. A multitude of problems forced the starport to be closed after a few centuries.
