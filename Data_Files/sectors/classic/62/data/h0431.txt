
Mercenaries from Answerin command premium prices for service.

Answerin culture holds fear of any kind to be a disease of the mind, and its psychiatrists have succeeded in eradicating all anxiety from the populace. The principal diagnostic tool for this is a portable device which detects anxiety by analyzing brain waves and autonomic physiological responses. The device borders too closely on psionic practices for legal export to the rest of the Imperium, but illegal copies are sometimes smuggled out.
