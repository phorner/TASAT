Yori

RS Beta: engages in long-range communications work. Salt mining from Yori’s huge dried sea beds is the planet's main industry; this salt is highly valued by the food industries of many worlds. In addition to Yori Startown, the other major settlement on the planet is Davinci. 

Ancient Site: A small Ancient site was found buried under shifting sands in a vast desert, surrounded by signs of destruction from the Final War.  Iridescent white building blocks and occasional deep, rubble-filled wells characterize this site.
