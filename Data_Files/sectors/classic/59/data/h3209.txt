Natoko

Natoko is largely undeveloped, and has a warm climate and a dense oxygen-nitrogen atmosphere.The world itself is owned and governed by Tukera Lines, which uses the starport to overhaul its space fleet. Essentially all inhabitants of Natoko are employees of Tukera.

In addition to Natoko Startown, the other major settlement on Natoko is Lakan City.
