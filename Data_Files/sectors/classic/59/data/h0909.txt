Gougeste

Gougeste is a nonindustrial hellworld with a population struggling to survive in an inhospitable environment. It requires extensive imports of outside technology to maintain a modern, star-faring society. This world is designated an "amber zone" and caution is advised. It is a dangerous world with an environment, laws, customs, life forms, or other conditions make it dangerous to visitors. 

It a client state of the Third Imperium in the Jewell Subsector of Spinward Marches Sector.
