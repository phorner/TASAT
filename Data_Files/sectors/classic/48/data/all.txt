Arzul sector. This sector is know as Ingukrax in the Two Thousand Worlds; also known as Mashidkhi in the First Imperium. 

This sector is part of the Lesser Rift. 

Arzul sector served as the birthplace of the Hegemony of Lorean, in 864 Imperial. The original capital of the Hegemony, Fathall (Arzul 0834), is also located here.
