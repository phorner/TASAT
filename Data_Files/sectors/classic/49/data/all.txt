Known to the Vargr as Gelath, known as Bariishra by the Vilani, and the K'Kree as Gn'hk'r, the sector eight sectors to trailing and one sector coreward of the Spinward Marches is notable for the jump-4 crossing of the Lesser Rift which connects the K'Kree Two Thousand Worlds with their outpost in the Vargr Extents.
