Kashaashii (Shadigi/Diaspora 0414)
D574471-5 S Ni               703 Na (Outlands)

Once a pleasant backwater with a slow, relaxed pace, Kashaashii is undergoing
a cottage industry boom which is keeping the planet from sliding back to a
pre-industrial technology level.

The local scout base continues operations, having been spared by the war.  The
base chief of operations, Irila Ajuknakar, has managed to inspire confidence 
among the locals, who look to him for leadership when they cannot reach a
consensus among themselves.
