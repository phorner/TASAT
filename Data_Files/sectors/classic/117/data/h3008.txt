Ningho (Khavle/Diaspora 3008)
E5771C8-7   Lo Ni            104 Na (Outlands)

The ten people on the planet are the remnants of a scientific team sent to
Ningho to take a census of and to study the intelligent native amphibians.
The natives are uninterested in technology or trade, and saw the attempt to
take a census as both silly and amusing.
