Lefiguura (Khulam/Diaspora 3132)
D868550-8 S Ag Ni            410 Na (Outlands)

The Scout base here is an island of TL11 efficiency, and has a live-and-let-live understanding with Solomani patrol units.  The base is supplied from theUnity of Promise (though the supply line is tenuous), and acts as "sky watchers" and liaisons to the Solomani for the Unity.
