Gashiila (Pasadaruu/Diaspora 3211)
E879465-5   Ni               614 Na (Outlands)

The planet ceased being balkanized when a battalion of grav cavalry troops from
the Vassalry Judiciate.  These troops were brought in by one of the factions
on-world in an attempt to win a local war.  The plan worked, but the Judiciate
troops declined to leave.

The Union of Sufren is supposedly ready and willing to commit a company of
mechanized infantry to expel the Judiciate troops, but is looking for private
ships and irregular units to support and lead the operation.
