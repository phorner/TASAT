Neto (Narquel/Diaspora 0504)
A300851-D   Na Va             912 Outlands

Neto supplies starships to Soyus and Gasudarsk, in support of their efforts
against the Ecclesiasty of Narquel.  The economy is dominated by the new
focus on shipyards, and several megacorporations have been invited to invest
in the planet to expand capabilities.  To date, interest has been expressed
by Ling Standard Products, General Products, and GsbAG.
