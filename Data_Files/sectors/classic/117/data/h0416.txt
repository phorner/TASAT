Terra Far (Shadigi/Diaspora 0416)
B4448BA-D                    113 Na (Outlands)

This world is a regular stop on the itinerary of almost every ship plying the 
spinward jump-1 section of the Shadigi Main.  Its class B starport makes it an
attractive place to have repairs and maintenance done.

In spite of efforts to quash it, Terra Far Down is the major distribution point
for the dangerous and highly addictive drug morandmor, made from the maratmor
plant native to Terra Far.
