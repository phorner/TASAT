Retinae

RsDelta:  the Imperium established a Research Station for communications research in this system in 556.  It has frequently figured in Zhodani diplomatic protests. It is supplied from Frenzie/Vilis through Thanber. 

The Imperium established a research station in the Retinae system in 556; the system was part of the Imperium then.  The Imperium maintains the station despite relinquishing the system (and all other possessions in the subsector) in 986.
