Thisbe 

A long-term project diverts large numbers of frozen water and gas asteroids from the Thisben Belt to the planetary surface. The intention is to improve the atmosphere and hydrosphere percentages. 

A desert world being terraformed by the megacorporation Sharurshid under a contract from the Ministry of Colonization.  Ice and frozen gas asteroids are maneuvered from the planetoid belts to Thisbe, where they improve water availability and atmosphere content.
