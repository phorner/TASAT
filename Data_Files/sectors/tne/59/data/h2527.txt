Pimane

Although the surface plains are baked vacuum, there is evidence the deep valleys on the surface contain both atmosphere and life.

The site of several small scientific research laboratories.  Recent investigations have located deep valleys which hold enough atmosphere to support indigenous life.  The current hypothesis is that the world originally had an atmosphere and supported life, but a cataclysmic collision with a rogue world stripped off Pimane's atmosphere several million years ago.
