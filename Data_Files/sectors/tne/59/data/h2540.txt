Aramis     
	Consists of a small industrial fief on long-term lease to Peabody Instrument, several coastal enclaves on the only moderately large continent of the planet, and a large outback, thinly populated with no government. 'Clave Detmender inspires the outbackers to increase their raids on several other enclaves.  'Clave Belcherfus hired a cadre.
