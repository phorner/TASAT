Classified Ads are 500x480 24-bit jpeg files (max 100 files per type: [S,V,I][1-100])

The sale of the day for each category is randomly generated when the TAS program starts based on the day and year.


Spacecraft filenames range from 1 to 100 and are prefixed with an "S".  Example:  S23.jpg

Vehicle filenames range from 1 to 100 and are prefixed with a "V".  Example: V100.jpg

Item filenames range from 1 to 100 and are prefixed with an "I".  Example: I2.jpg
